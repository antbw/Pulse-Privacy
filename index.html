Pulse Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

Overview

Pulse is a personal health tracking application designed for individual use. This policy explains how data is handled when you use Pulse.

Data Collection

Pulse connects to third-party health platforms (including Whoop, Oura, and Apple Health) to retrieve your personal health and activity data, including heart rate, HRV, sleep metrics, and activity data. This data is collected solely at your direction.

Data Storage

All health data retrieved by Pulse is stored locally on your personal device. No data is transmitted to or stored on external servers operated by Pulse.

Third-Party Services

Pulse connects to third-party APIs (such as Whoop) using credentials you provide. Your use of those platforms is governed by their respective privacy policies.

Data Sharing

Pulse does not sell, share, or transmit your personal health data to any third parties.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact: anthony@pulseapp.com
